Fyde

Description:

Cultivating partnerships: helping Fyde on their way to a trustless crypto management revolution with an effective and beautiful partnership deck (sales deck).

Company

About:

Fyde, from London UK, revolutionizes DeFi for crypto companies globally, unlocking their balance sheet’s full potential.

Industry:
Blockchain Services, Crypto Web3

Company Size:
10 people

Challenge & Solution

Services we provided:

Challenge:

Fyde’s intricate, comprehensive product aimed to cover diverse treasury needs. However, the partnership’s presentation lacked clarity, leaving slides puzzling and questions unresolved.

The existing presentation was quite complex, filled with verbose and repetitive content, and didn’t align with the speaker’s message, which unfortunately hindered Fyde’s ability to make a strong initial impression and show the product’s potential to the fullest.

Solution:

Our approach involved a deep dive into both the industry and the company itself. Our goal: crystal-clear slides that conveyed key points cohesively.

Seeking valuable insights by analyzing past company presentations and relevant documents, while exploring the market’s depths.

We broke down Fyde’s intricate processes into easy-to-grasp concepts and created visuals to simplify the protocol’s explanation, aiming for both consistency and intuitive understanding.

A story powered by data was built to illustrate product depths and partner value, leading to a transition from confusion to clarity.
